Privacy Policy

Target App: Hidden Octopus

Operator (Publisher): OwnReview

Effective date: June 23, 2026

This Privacy Policy (the "Policy") sets out how the App handles information about its users. It explains what information the App collects, how that information is used and shared, and how users can manage their own information.

Important Statement
We do not sell users' personal information to third parties. The App is intended for users of all ages, but it is not primarily directed at children under 13. We treat the protection of your privacy as our highest priority so that you can use the App with peace of mind.

1. Contact

For any inquiries regarding how the App handles information, please contact us at:

  • Operator: OwnReview
  • Contact: contact@ownreview.jp

2. Information We Collect

The App does not collect information that directly identifies an individual, such as your name, email address, phone number, or date of birth. The App has no account registration feature and no posting feature. The information the App handles is limited to the following.

(1) Information automatically collected through advertising (Google AdMob)

  • Device identifiers (advertising ID: IDFA on iOS / AAID on Android)
  • Device information (OS, model, language settings, etc.)
  • Approximate location (country/region level, based on IP address)
  • Information about the display of and interaction with advertisements

(2) Information related to the online multiplayer feature (Unity Gaming Services)

  • An anonymous authentication ID (an anonymous identifier issued automatically by the service, not linked to your name, email, etc.)
  • Temporary connection information for multiplayer connectivity (used only during a match session)

3. Purposes of Use

We use the information we collect only for the following purposes.

  • (1) To display and deliver advertisements within the App
  • (2) To measure advertising effectiveness
  • (3) To provide the online multiplayer feature
  • (4) To investigate defects and improve the quality of the App
  • (5) To ensure the safety of the App and prevent fraudulent use

4. Provision of Information to Third Parties

Except as permitted by law, we do not sell or provide information to third parties without the user's consent. The App uses the following third-party services to provide its functionality, and the information described above is transmitted to them.

  • Google AdMob (advertising)
    • AdMob / ad-related privacy: https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads
    • Google Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy
  • Unity Gaming Services (multiplayer)
    • Unity Privacy Policy: https://unity.com/legal/privacy-policy

5. International Data Transfers

Information collected by the App may be stored and processed on servers of overseas companies such as Google and Unity in order to provide the services. These companies comply with appropriate data protection standards.

6. Advertising and Tracking

  • The App may display personalized advertisements based on your interests.
  • For iOS users: When the App launches, an App Tracking Transparency ("Allow Tracking") prompt is shown. Even if you do not allow tracking, you can still use the App, and advertisements will be shown in a non-personalized form.
  • For regions such as the EU/EEA and the UK: In accordance with applicable laws (such as the GDPR), a consent message regarding advertising (Google's User Messaging Platform) is displayed. You can change your consent settings at any time.
  • You can opt out of personalized advertising at any time from your device settings (after opting out, ads will still be shown but will be generic ads not based on your interests).
    • iOS: Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking
    • Android: Settings → Google → Ads

7. Data Retention

  • The App itself does not store user information on our servers (we do not operate a dedicated server for the App).
  • The retention period of advertising and multiplayer information follows the policies of each third-party service (Google / Unity).

8. Child Safety (Standards against Child Sexual Abuse and Exploitation (CSAE))

We treat the safety and protection of children as our highest priority. The App expressly prohibits any content or conduct related to child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE).

  • The App is not primarily directed at users under 13 and has no feature for users to post or share content.
  • In consideration of child protection laws such as the U.S. COPPA and the EU GDPR, the App delivers advertising only in a non-personalized form (generic ads not based on interests). Accordingly, we do not perform behavioral targeting advertising toward users under 13.
  • We do not intentionally collect personal information from children under 13. If it comes to our attention that information of a child under 13 has been collected without parental consent, we will make efforts to promptly delete such information.
  • For concerns or reports regarding CSAE, please contact us at the contact point in Section 1. Where legally required, we will report to the relevant authorities.

9. Disclosure, Correction, and Deletion of Information

If a user requests disclosure, correction, deletion, or similar handling of information, we will respond in accordance with the law after verifying the identity of the requester. Please direct such requests to the contact point in Section 1.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may revise this Policy as necessary. If there are material changes, we will announce them within the App or on this page. If you use the App after the changes, you are deemed to have agreed to the revised Policy.
